Interaction Write-up of this example for the UMN interaction field

Under Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), a person is not required to bring their own interpreter to a healthcare visit. Instead, the law requires that the doctor’s office takes steps to help with communication. This would include providing an interpreter in the language needed. Although, if a person chooses to they can bring along a family member or other person to help with communication.
